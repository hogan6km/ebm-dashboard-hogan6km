## Evidence Quality Assessment - MILESTONE 2 ANALYSIS

### Consistency Across Sources
#### Consistent Messages
- **Organizational Stability Critical:** Both sources emphasize need for predictable, stable work environment
- **Culture Over Compensation:** Restaurant Business and Tanveer both identify workplace culture as key retention factor
- **Employee Voice Important:** Both sources stress importance of giving employees input in organizational changes
- **Leadership Accountability:** Both sources identify leadership practices as primary driver of turnover

#### Inconsistent or Conflicting Advice
- **Timeline Expectations:** Tanveer suggests immediate impact possible, Restaurant Business indicates longer-term cultural change required
- **Change Management:** Tanveer advocates for limiting changes, industry report suggests need for systematic transformation
- **Implementation Focus:** Tanveer emphasizes protecting existing processes, industry analysis suggests need for new approaches

#### Pattern Recognition
- **Root Cause Agreement:** Both sources identify organizational chaos/instability as primary turnover driver
- **Solution Convergence:** Focus on employee development, culture improvement, and leadership accountability
- **Industry-Wide Challenge:** Both confirm turnover as systematic industry problem requiring systematic solutions

### Depth and Specificity Assessment
#### Detailed vs. General Advice
- **HIGHLY SPECIFIC:** Tanveer provides exact metrics (94% adoption, 22% quota improvement), specific timelines (4-week stabilization periods)
- **ACTIONABLE FRAMEWORKS:** Clear implementation steps: "quarterly change calendar," "stabilization sprints," structured voice collection
- **CONCRETE EXAMPLES:** Real case study of killing three initiatives with measurable outcomes

#### Evidence-Based vs. Opinion-Based
- **DATA-BACKED:** Tanveer provides specific performance metrics before/after implementation
- **INDUSTRY EVIDENCE:** Restaurant Business cites industry-wide trends and systematic analysis
- **MEASURABLE OUTCOMES:** Both sources reference quantifiable results rather than anecdotal impressions
- **LONGITUDINAL PERSPECTIVE:** 20+ years experience (Tanveer) and multi-year industry trends (Restaurant Business)

#### Implementation Detail
- **COMPREHENSIVE GUIDANCE:** Step-by-step frameworks for organizational stability and change management
- **PRACTICAL TOOLS:** Specific tools like "quarterly change calendar" and "stabilization sprint" methodology
- **REALISTIC TIMELINES:** Both sources provide realistic expectations for implementation and results
- **RESOURCE REQUIREMENTS:** Clear guidance on leadership discipline and organizational capacity needed
