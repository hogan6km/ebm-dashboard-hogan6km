## Selection Criteria Applied

- **Study Design:** Quantitative studies with measurable effect sizes (correlations, Cohen's d, odds ratios)
- **Population:** Hospitality industry employees (hotels, restaurants, tourism)
- **Interventions:** Career development, training programs, HR retention strategies  
- **Outcomes:** Turnover rates, retention rates, intention to quit, job satisfaction
- **Quality:** Peer-reviewed, adequate sample size (n>100), clear methodology

### Exclusion Criteria Applied
- Studies without quantifiable effect sizes
- Non-hospitality industries (unless directly comparable)
- Purely qualitative studies (saved for practitioner evidence)
- Studies older than 15 years (pre-2010)
- Non-English publications
- Conference abstracts without full papers
