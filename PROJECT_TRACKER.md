## üéØ **PROJECT OVERVIEW**

### **Management Problem**
**Problem Statement:** How can retail organizations reduce voluntary employee turnover from 35% to industry best-practice levels (15-20%) while maintaining service quality and operational efficiency?

**Organization Context:** Mid-size regional retail chain with multiple locations experiencing high turnover costs and service quality impacts

**Why This Matters:** High turnover creates cascading effects including recruitment costs ($480K annually), training expenses, reduced customer satisfaction, and operational disruption

**Success Vision:** Sustainable retention strategy achieving 40% reduction in voluntary turnover, $300K annual cost savings, and improved customer satisfaction through consistent service delivery
